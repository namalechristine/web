Welcome to Uganda Martyrs University

Applications for the next intake are open

Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) is a faith-based private University owned by the Episcopal Conference of the Catholic Bishops of Uganda. UMU acknowledges the late Archbishop Kiwanuka as the Father of Catholic higher education in Uganda.

The Academic Registrar, Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) invites prospective applicants from Uganda, East Africa, and around the world for Postgraduate and Undergraduate programs in the current intake. The University currently provides three avenues for admission, including direct entry, entry for diploma holders (both graduate and postgraduate), and admission through the web portal.
